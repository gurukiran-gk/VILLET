  2. Due Date: The cardholder is required to make the minimum payment amount indicated on the monthly billing statement by the specified due date. The due date will be clearly mentioned on the statement.

  3. Late Payment Charges: If the payment is not received by the due date, a late payment charge of 0.5% of the outstanding balance will be imposed. This charge will be applicable for the period from the due date until the payment is received.

  4. Grace Period: A grace period of 15 days is provided after the due date. During this period, if the payment is made, only a penalty charge of 2% of the outstanding balance will be applied.

  5. Extended Late Payment: If the payment is not received within the grace period of 15 days, an extended late payment charge of 4% of the outstanding balance will be imposed. This charge will be applicable for the next 15-day period.

  6. Monthly Late Payment: If the payment remains overdue beyond the extended late payment period, a monthly late payment charge of 12% of the outstanding balance will be applied for every subsequent one-month period until the payment is received.

  7. Minimum Payment: The cardholder is required to make a minimum payment each month as stated on the billing statement. Failure to make the minimum payment may result in additional fees and penalties.
